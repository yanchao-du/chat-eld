# Voter Registration

- **Eligibility:** You must be a Singapore citizen, aged 21 or above as of the cut-off date, and not disqualified under any written law.
- **Automatic Registration:** Eligible citizens are automatically registered as electors.
- **Checking Status:** You can check your voter status online at the ELD website or via the Singpass app.
- **Address Updates:** Your registered address follows your NRIC address. Ensure you update your address with ICA within 28 days of moving.
