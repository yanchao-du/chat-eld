# FAQ

- **What are the voting hours?**
  Polling stations are open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on Polling Day.

- **What ID is required?**
  You must bring your original NRIC or valid passport, along with your official poll card.

- **Is voting compulsory?**
  Yes, voting is compulsory for all eligible citizens in Singapore.

- **What if I am sick on Polling Day?**
  If you have a valid medical certificate or are hospitalized, your name may be restored to the registers of electors subsequently without penalty.
