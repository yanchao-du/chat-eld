# How to Vote

1. **Receive Poll Card:** You will receive your poll card via mail or you can retrieve the e-Poll card via the Singpass app.
2. **Go to Polling Station:** Visit your designated polling station stated on your poll card between 8 AM and 8 PM on Polling Day.
3. **Registration:** Present your NRIC/Passport and poll card to the election official for verification.
4. **Collect Ballot:** Receive your ballot paper.
5. **Mark Ballot:** Go to a voting booth and mark your choice clearly with an 'X' in the empty box beside the candidate/party of your choice.
6. **Cast Vote:** Fold the ballot paper and drop it into the ballot box before leaving the station.
