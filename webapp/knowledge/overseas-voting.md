# Overseas Voting

- **Eligibility:** Singapore citizens living abroad can register as overseas voters if they have resided in Singapore for at least 30 days in the 3-year period before the cut-off date.
- **Registration:** Registration must be done online before the writ of election is issued.
- **Voting Methods:** 
  - **Overseas Polling Stations:** Vote in person at designated overseas polling stations (e.g., Embassies, High Commissions).
  - **Postal Voting:** Registered overseas voters can download the postal ballot paper via Singpass and mail it back.
