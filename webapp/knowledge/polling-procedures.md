# Polling Procedures

- **Secrecy of the Ballot:** Your vote is completely secret. The ballot papers are traced only if ordered by the court for election petitions.
- **Rejected Ballots:** A ballot paper is rejected if it has more than one mark, identifying marks, or is blank.
- **Assistance:** Visually impaired voters can request a ballot template. Seniors or those needing physical assistance can be accompanied by an election official.
